About Me

Hello there!

I'm a 26-year-old tech enthusiast from São Bernardo do Campo, fueled by my love for games, technology, and art — oh, and of course, my feline overlord, Anubis. 🐈‍⬛💻🎮

Right now, I'm diving deep into the world of Software Analysis and Development at FIAP, while also sharpening my skills in both Front-end and Back-end development. My mission? To build an awesome portfolio that not only showcases my projects but also reflects my passion for creativity and innovation.


I'm always up for new challenges, whether it's coding something cool, geeking out over a new game, or just trying to get Anubis to respect my keyboard space. (Still a work in progress.) 😼✨

Ever since I was around six, playing video games with my dad, technology has been my constant companion. What started as gaming adventures evolved into a deeper curiosity about how digital experiences were created (despite those unavoidable pauses that adulthood brings).


In school, I was searching for my path, floating through subjects until I found psychology!
The human mind fascinated me — I devoured books on cognitive processes, behavioral patterns, and user experiences (analyzing people, but not in a creepy way!). College immersed me in understanding how people think and interact with their environment. After graduating, my HR career taught me valuable insights about user needs and team dynamics, though I sensed something was still missing.


Now, I'm diving deep into Front-end and Back-end development. Technology, software, and creating things are what truly drive me. I'm excited about learning, growing, and, most importantly, using tech to make people's lives better. It feels like I'm finally on the right path, blending my love for tech with my desire to help others. I'm combining my analytical thinking from psychology with technical skills in development, creating solutions that are not just functional but truly resonate with people!

I'm looking forward to working on new projects and constantly learning new things!


The journey never stops, and I'm more excited than ever to dive into fresh challenges and innovations. Feel free to check out what I'm developing — I'm always up to something new! 🚀
